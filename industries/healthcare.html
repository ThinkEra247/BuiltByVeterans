FAQ

Common Questions from Healthcare Organizations

How do you ensure HIPAA compliance?

We implement comprehensive security controls including encryption, access management, audit logging, network segmentation, and Business Associate Agreements. We also conduct regular risk assessments and maintain compliance documentation.

Do you support Epic and eClinicalWorks?

Yes. We have extensive experience supporting Epic and eClinicalWorks deployments, including server management, performance optimization, updates, and integration with labs, imaging, and billing systems.

Can you support telehealth platforms?

We configure and maintain secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth infrastructure including network optimization, video quality assurance, and integration with your EHR system.

How do backups work?

We implement automated, encrypted backups that run continuously. Patient records, EHR databases, and clinical files are backed up both locally and to HIPAA-compliant cloud storage with rapid recovery options.

Do you offer after-hours support?

Yes. Our 24/7 monitoring means we catch issues around the clock, and our support team is available for emergencies outside normal business hours—critical for organizations providing patient care.

How long does onboarding take?

Most healthcare organizations are fully onboarded within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. We plan transitions carefully to avoid any disruption to patient care or clinical operations.

Can you manage medical devices on our network?

Yes. We implement secure network segmentation for medical devices, manage firmware updates, monitor device connectivity, and ensure IoT medical equipment doesn't create security vulnerabilities.

What if we already have an IT provider?

We handle the transition seamlessly. We coordinate with your existing provider and ensure nothing falls through the cracks during the switch—with zero impact on patient care.
