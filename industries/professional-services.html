FAQ

Common Questions from Professional Service Firms

How do you protect client data?

We implement layered security including endpoint protection, encrypted storage, network segmentation, multi-factor authentication, and strict access controls to ensure client data remains secure across all systems.

Do you support Microsoft 365?

Yes. We are Microsoft 365 experts—handling deployment, migration, security configuration, Teams optimization, SharePoint setup, and ongoing administration for professional service firms.

Can you help with cloud migration?

We plan and execute cloud migrations to Azure, Microsoft 365, and other platforms with minimal disruption. We handle data migration, application compatibility, security configuration, and user training.

How do backups work?

We implement automated, encrypted backups that run continuously. Client files, project data, email, and business-critical systems are backed up both locally and to the cloud with rapid recovery options.

Do you offer after-hours support?

Yes. Our 24/7 monitoring means we catch issues around the clock, and our support team is available for emergencies outside normal business hours—critical for firms with client deadlines.

How long does onboarding take?

Most professional service firms are fully onboarded within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. We plan transitions carefully to avoid any disruption to client deliverables or team productivity.

How do you support remote teams?

We configure secure remote access through VPN, Zero Trust architecture, cloud desktops, and optimized Microsoft Teams environments so your team can work productively from anywhere.

What if we already have an IT provider?

We handle the transition seamlessly. We coordinate with your existing provider and ensure nothing falls through the cracks during the switch—with zero impact on client work.
