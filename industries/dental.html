FAQ

Common Questions from Dental Practices

How do you protect patient data?

We use layered security including endpoint protection, encrypted backups, network segmentation, and access controls to ensure patient data remains secure and HIPAA-compliant.

Do you support Open Dental?

Yes. We have extensive experience supporting Open Dental installations, including server management, database optimization, updates, and integrations with imaging systems.

Can you manage imaging servers?

We manage CBCT, Dexis, Carestream, and other imaging systems including storage, backups, and network performance optimization.

How do backups work?

We implement automated, encrypted backups that run continuously. Patient data, imaging files, and practice management databases are backed up both locally and to the cloud.

Do you offer after-hours support?

Yes. Our 24/7 monitoring means we catch issues around the clock, and our support team is available for emergencies outside normal business hours.

How long does onboarding take?

Most dental practices are fully onboarded within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. We plan transitions carefully to avoid any disruption to patient care.

Do you help with HIPAA compliance?

Yes. We provide HIPAA security risk assessments, policy documentation, staff training recommendations, and ongoing compliance monitoring.

What if we already have an IT provider?

We handle the transition seamlessly. We coordinate with your existing provider and ensure nothing falls through the cracks during the switch.
