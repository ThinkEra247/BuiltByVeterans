FAQ

Common Questions from Law Firms

How do you protect client data and confidentiality?

We implement layered security including endpoint protection, encrypted communications, network segmentation, ethical wall configurations, and strict access controls to ensure attorney-client privilege is maintained.

Do you support Clio?

Yes. We have extensive experience supporting Clio installations, including integration with Microsoft 365, document management workflows, and ensuring reliable cloud connectivity for your team.

Can you help with document management?

We support NetDocuments, SharePoint, and other DMS platforms including setup, migration, access controls, and integration with your practice management software.

How do you handle ethical walls?

We configure information barriers at the system level—restricting file access, email routing, and application permissions to maintain strict separation between conflicting matters.

Do you offer after-hours support?

Yes. Our 24/7 monitoring means we catch issues around the clock, and our support team is available for emergencies outside normal business hours—critical for firms working against court deadlines.

How long does onboarding take?

Most law firms are fully onboarded within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. We plan transitions carefully to avoid any disruption to active cases or court deadlines.

Do you help with compliance requirements?

Yes. We provide security assessments, data handling policy documentation, staff training recommendations, and ongoing monitoring to help meet bar association and regulatory compliance standards.

What if we already have an IT provider?

We handle the transition seamlessly. We coordinate with your existing provider and ensure nothing falls through the cracks during the switch—with zero impact on active casework.
