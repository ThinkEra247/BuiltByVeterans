FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Managed IT?

Managed IT means outsourcing the management of your entire technology environment to a trusted partner. We handle everything from help desk support and workstation management to cybersecurity, cloud solutions, and vendor coordination—so your team can focus on business growth instead of technology headaches.

How quickly do you respond?

Our average response time is under 15 minutes for critical issues. All tickets are triaged immediately and our U.S.-based support team is available during extended business hours with 24/7 monitoring for emergencies.

Do you replace our internal IT staff?

We can function as your complete IT department or work alongside your existing staff. Many clients use us as their full technology team, while others use us to augment internal resources and provide specialized expertise their team doesn't have.

Can you support multiple offices?

Yes. We support businesses across multiple locations with remote monitoring, management tools, and on-site support coordination. Our cloud-first approach means your team stays connected regardless of where they work.

Do you support Microsoft 365?

Absolutely. We are a Microsoft partner and manage Microsoft 365 environments including Exchange, Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive, Intune, and Azure AD. We handle licensing, security configuration, user provisioning, and ongoing administration.

How are backups tested?

We perform automated daily backup verification and conduct regular test restores to confirm data integrity. Our team reviews backup reports and alerts to ensure your business can recover quickly in any scenario.

Can you work with our existing vendors?

Yes—vendor coordination is one of our biggest differentiators. We work directly with your internet provider, phone provider, software vendors, hardware manufacturers, and any other technology partner on your behalf. One call to us handles everything.

What happens if our internet provider has an outage?

We monitor your connectivity in real time. If an outage occurs, we immediately open a ticket with your internet provider, coordinate the resolution, and keep your team informed with status updates until service is fully restored.

How does onboarding work?

When you hire a new employee, simply let us know. We provision their accounts, configure their workstation, set up email and applications, assign security permissions, and ensure they are productive from day one.

How does offboarding work?

When an employee departs, we disable their accounts, revoke access, transfer data ownership, archive mailboxes, and wipe devices according to your security policies. This protects your business from data loss and unauthorized access.
