FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ransomware?

Ransomware is malicious software that encrypts your files and demands payment to unlock them. It can spread across your network in minutes, affecting servers, workstations, and backups. Early detection and layered security are the most effective defenses against it.

How is ransomware detected?

We use behavior-based detection that monitors for unusual activity like rapid file encryption, suspicious login patterns, privilege escalation, and lateral movement across your network. This approach catches threats that traditional antivirus often misses entirely.

What happens if my computer becomes infected?

Our system automatically isolates the affected endpoint to prevent the threat from spreading. Our security team investigates the incident, removes the threat, and restores your systems from verified backups. We keep you informed throughout the entire process.

How quickly do you respond?

Critical security alerts are triaged immediately. Automated containment happens within seconds of detection. Our security team investigates and responds to confirmed threats within minutes, not hours.

Do you monitor after business hours?

Yes. Our monitoring runs 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Cyber threats don't follow a schedule, so neither does our protection. Your business is monitored continuously regardless of the time or day.

How do backups protect my business?

Verified backups ensure that even in a worst-case scenario, your data can be restored quickly. We test backups regularly, store them in secure locations, and maintain recovery procedures so your business can get back to normal with minimal data loss.

Can remote employees be protected?

Yes. Our endpoint detection and response tools protect devices regardless of location. Remote employees receive the same level of monitoring and protection whether they work from home, a coffee shop, or a client site.

Do you support Microsoft 365 security?

Yes. We configure and manage Microsoft 365 security features including Advanced Threat Protection, Safe Attachments, Safe Links, anti-phishing policies, and conditional access to protect your email and cloud environment.

Will security slow down my computers?

No. Modern endpoint detection tools are lightweight and designed to run efficiently in the background. Your team won't notice any performance impact during normal daily operations.

How often are systems updated?

We apply security patches and updates on a regular schedule—typically weekly for standard updates and immediately for critical vulnerabilities. Updates are tested and deployed during low-impact windows to avoid disruption.
